About To-Do List

To-Do List helps to sort your work and life into simple categories. Create as many categories as you want, add tasks under them, and mark them when done.

Turn on “ Edit” mode anytime to rename, delete, or reorder your categories easily.
Use the “+ Category” button to create your own sections, and toggle “Edit” mode to rename or remove categories.

You can also add dates to your tasks!
Try the Calendar to plan with your tasks here.
